Heartopia is a shared world where up to **11 other players** can be visible at any time. This guide covers every multiplayer feature — from adding your first friend to building a house together.

---

## How Multiplayer Works

- Up to **11 other players** are visible in your world at any time
- Players appear as they go about their own activities — fishing, gardening, exploring
- Chat is **only with confirmed friends** — no forced public chat with strangers
- The world feels alive but never overwhelming

---

## Friendship Journal

Unlocked at **DG Level 4**. This is your friends list and the hub for all social features.

### How to Add a Friend

1. Open your **Friendship Journal** from the smartwatch
2. Enter your friend's **UID** (unique player ID)
3. Sending a friend request costs **1 Wishing Star**
4. Once accepted, your friend appears in your journal

### Friend Benefits

- Visit each other's towns for **free**
- Send **gifts** while a friend is offline (they arrive in their mailbox)
- **Co-Build** together (DG Level 8+)
- Chat freely anywhere

### How to Find Your UID

Your UID is displayed in the Friendship Journal and in your smartwatch settings. Share it with friends so they can add you.

---

## Visiting Friends

1. Open your Friendship Journal
2. Select a friend who is online
3. Choose **"Visit Town"**
4. You'll teleport to their world

While visiting, you can explore their home, see their decorations, and interact with their town. You can't modify their home unless they grant you Co-Build permissions.

## Coffee Umbrellas

Coffee Umbrellas are designated social spots scattered around Heartopia. They're marked with a coffee cup icon.

### What You Can Do at Coffee Umbrellas

- **Chat freely** with up to 3 other players at the same table
- **Play puzzles** together — cooperative mini-games
- **Sea-faring fishing quests** — launch fishing trips together
- Relax and take photos

Coffee Umbrellas are the primary way to socialize with players who aren't on your friends list. Sit down at an open table and see who joins.

## Co-Building

Unlocked at **DG Level 8**. Build together with a friend in real-time.

### How to Start Co-Building

1. Create a **dedicated blueprint slot** for co-building (never use your main house slot)
2. Open your Friendship Journal and select a friend
3. Choose **"Invite to Co-Build"**
4. Grant build permissions on the dedicated slot only

### Co-Build Safety Rules

| Rule | Why |
| --- | --- |
| Use a dedicated blueprint slot | Prevents accidental damage to your main house |
| Only co-build with trusted friends | Build permissions can be abused |
| Screenshot your build before starting | Easy rollback reference |
| Revoke permissions when done | Clean end to the session |

### What You Can Do Together

- Place, move, and rotate furniture simultaneously
- Brainstorm and execute complex designs faster
- Share furniture and material ideas
- Preview each other's changes in real-time

## Party Feature

Unlocked at **DG Level 10**. Host gatherings at your home.

### How to Host a Party

1. Open your smartwatch and go to the Party menu
2. Choose a party type (casual, themed, etc.)
3. Invite friends from your Friendship Journal
4. Friends teleport to your home

### Party Activities

- Chat and socialize
- Show off your latest home build
- Play music if you have instruments
- Host a **Tea Party** — no DG level required, host from home

---

## Sea Fishing with Friends

Unlocked at **DG Level 7**. Bill's boat can take you and your party out to sea.

1. Talk to **Bill** at Fishing Village docks
2. Choose **Sea Fishing Event**
3. Invite friends from your party
4. Costs **2,000 Gold** per session (3,000 for repeats)
5. Catch rare high-value fish together
6. Complete group goals for Gatcha Eggs

## Cross-Platform Play

Heartopia supports **full cross-platform play** between mobile and PC:

| Platform | How to Play |
| --- | --- |
| **iOS** | App Store |
| **Android** | Google Play |
| **PC (Steam)** | Steam (free-to-play) |

### Cross-Platform Facts

- **Mobile and PC players share the same servers** — you can play with anyone regardless of their device
- **Progress syncs across devices** — log in on PC, continue on mobile, all progress is saved
- **Steam is region-locked for China** — Chinese IPs cannot access Heartopia on Steam. Use the mobile version instead.
- Use the same account to log in on any device

## Chat System

### How Chat Works

- Chat is **only enabled with confirmed friends**
- No public global chat — you won't see messages from strangers
- At Coffee Umbrellas, you can chat with up to 3 table-mates
- During co-building, chat is always available with your building partner

### Emotes & Expressions

- Unlocked at **DG Level 3**
- Purchase from **Annie** (basic emotes) and event shops (limited emotes)
- Use emotes to communicate without typing
- The March 2026 update added a **"tap to get attention"** feature

## Multiplayer Etiquette

1. **Ask before visiting** — a quick message before dropping into someone's town
2. **Don't touch without permission** — never interact with another player's furniture or crops unless invited
3. **Respect co-build boundaries** — if you're given build permissions, stay within the agreed scope
4. **Tip your host** — if someone hosts a great party, send a small gift afterward
5. **Coffee Umbrellas are public spaces** — be friendly to strangers who sit down
