Heartopia has **7 hobbies** that form the core gameplay loop. Each hobby is unlocked with a **Hobby Expansion Ticket** earned from DG level-ups. This guide covers the best unlock order, how to level up each hobby, and profit strategies.

```
Level 2:  Fishing       (forced)

```

## Fishing

**Mentor**: Vanya  
**Unlock**: DG Level 2 (mandatory)  
**Upgrade**: Hobby Upgrade Tickets + accumulating fishing proficiency

### How It Works

Equip your fishing rod, find a body of water, and cast. Different fish spawn at different locations, times, and weather conditions.

### Key Fish Species

| Fish | Location | Time | Raw Value | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Crucian Carp | Spring Lake | All day | ~80g | Beginner-friendly, consistent income |
| Rainbow Fish | Starlight Sea | Rainbow weather | Very high | Requires premium bait |
| Mermaid Fish | Sea Fishing event | Event only | High | Event currency + gold |

### Sea Fishing Event

- Unlocks at **DG Level 7**
- Talk to **Bill** at Fishing Village docks
- Costs **2,000 Gold** per session (3,000 for repeats)
- Solo or party play
- Catch unique high-value fish and earn Gatcha eggs

### Fishing Tips

- **Always carry bait** (3 Apples per craft) — speeds up catches
- **Match fish to conditions** — rare fish spawn during specific weather/windows
- **Cook before selling** — Fried Crucian Carp = 200g vs. 80g raw
- **Use repair kits** — fishing rods wear down with use

### Upgrade Priority: #1

Fishing is your strongest early-game income. Upgrade it first with Hobby Upgrade Tickets.

## Gardening

**Mentor**: Blanc  
**Unlock**: DG Level 3 (mandatory)  
**Upgrade**: Hobby Upgrade Tickets + accumulating gardening proficiency

### How It Works

Buy planters and seeds from Blanc. Plant, water, fertilize, and harvest crops. Crops feed all cooking recipes.

### Full Crop List

| Crop | Grow Time | Source | Best Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tomato | 15 min | Blanc | Quick turnover, salads |
| Pineapple | 30 min | Blanc | Fast gold while active |
| Wheat | 4 hours | Blanc | Bread, cakes, pasta |
| Strawberry | 6 hours | Blanc | Strawberry Jam — up to 12,640g at 5-star |
| Corn | 12 hours | Blanc | Corn Soup (~400g), overnight planting |
| Weed | Random | Clearing fields | Fertilizer (4 Weeds + 3 Timber) |

### Gardening Tips

- **Water immediately after planting** — speeds growth by ~15 min per watering
- **Fertilize immediately after planting** (not mid-growth) for full quality benefit
- **Hold-to-water-multi** lets you water 3-5 plots at once
- **Corn + Strawberry rotation**: corn overnight (12h), strawberries during active play (6h)
- Crops don't die without water — growth just pauses

### Upgrade Priority: #2

Gardening is the backbone of cooking. Without good crops, you can't cook high-value dishes.

## Cooking

**Mentor**: Massimo  
**Unlock**: DG Level 5 (recommended)  
**Upgrade**: Hobby Upgrade Tickets + accumulating cooking proficiency

### How It Works

Use stoves to combine ingredients into dishes. Cooked dishes sell for **3-10× the raw ingredient value**. This is the single best money-maker in Heartopia.

### Top Profit Recipes

| Dish | Ingredients | Sell Price | Profit Margin |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Strawberry Jam | 4x Strawberry | 160-12,640g | Massive at 5-star |
| Apple Jam | 4x Apple | ~160g | Apples are free from trees |
| Corn Soup | 1x Corn | ~400g | Plant overnight, cook in morning |
| Fish & Chips | Fish + Potato | ~300g | Consistent mid-game income |
| Black Truffle Pie | 2x Black Truffle + Wheat + Egg | Very high | Late-game gold farm |
| Raspberry Jam | 4x Raspberry | ~200g | Easy forage → cook loop |

### Batch Cooking Setup

1. Buy **4 stoves** from Bob's Furniture Store
2. Arrange them in a **tight square**
3. Stand in the **middle** of the square
4. You can now manage all 4 stoves simultaneously — 4× cooking speed

### Cooking Tips

- **Never sell raw if cooking 2×+ the value** — but check: a 320g fish in a 310g dish loses money
- **Cook identical mushrooms together** (4 Oyster Mushrooms) to unlock secret recipe variants
- **Massimo sells Milk** (200g) and **Matcha Powder** (300g) — key for premium recipes
- Grass Cake: Wheat + Milk + Matcha Powder + Weed

### Upgrade Priority: #3

Fishing and Gardening feed Cooking. Once those two are solid, pump Cooking for exponential gold.

## Insect Catching

**Mentor**: Naniwa  
**Unlock**: DG Level 5, 8, or 11 (choose with ticket)  
**Upgrade**: Hobby Upgrade Tickets + proficiency

### How It Works

Equip a bug net and approach insects in the wild. Different species appear by region and time of day.

### Key Insects

| Insect | Region | Time | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Butterflies | Flower Field | Daytime | Common, easy catches |
| Beetles | Forest | Night | Higher value |
| Dragonflies | Near water | Daytime | Medium value |
| Fireflies | Suburban areas | Night | Seasonal, event-linked |

### Insect Catching Tips

- Sell catches to **Albert Jr.** for gold
- Some insects are used in cooking recipes
- The **Bait the Insects** event unlocks at DG Level 8 (requires Insect Catching)

### Upgrade Priority: #4

Solid supplementary income but nowhere near Cooking. Upgrade after the Big Three.

## Birdwatching

**Mentor**: Bailey J  
**Unlock**: DG Level 5, 8, or 11 (choose with ticket — save for last)  
**Upgrade**: Hobby Upgrade Tickets + proficiency

### How It Works

Spot birds in the wild and collect **bird cards**. Each bird species has its own card.

### Bird Card Turn-In

- Give **5 bird cards/day** to **Bailey J**
- Rewards: **Repair Kits** and crafting materials
- This is the primary reason to do Birdwatching — free repair kits

### Birdwatching Event

- **Nest of Hundreds** unlocks at DG Level 8 (requires Birdwatching)
- Special event with unique rewards

### Upgrade Priority: #7 (Last)

Birdwatching has the least economic value. Turn in your 5 daily cards for repair kits, but don't invest upgrade tickets here until everything else is maxed.

## Cat Caring

**Mentor**: Mrs. Joan  
**Unlock**: DG Level 12 (first of two tickets at this level)  
**Upgrade**: Hobby Upgrade Tickets + proficiency

### How It Works

Adopt a cat from Mrs. Joan's Pet Shop. Feed, play with, and train your cat daily. Each interaction earns gold and items — rewards arrive in your mailbox the next day.

### Cat Care Tips

- Feed daily for consistent gold rewards
- Pet costumes available at Mrs. Joan's (Hip Hop Set, Banana Head added March 2026)
- You must unlock Cat Caring before Dog Caring

### Upgrade Priority: #5

Pets provide passive daily income. Unlock Cat first, then Dog.

---

## Dog Caring

**Mentor**: Mrs. Joan  
**Unlock**: DG Level 12 (requires Cat Caring completed first)  
**Upgrade**: Hobby Upgrade Tickets + proficiency

### How It Works

Same as Cat Caring — adopt, feed, play, train daily. Rewards arrive in your mailbox the next day. Dogs have different costume and interaction options.

### Upgrade Priority: #6

Unlock after Cat. Both pets together provide steady passive income with minimal daily effort.
