Heartopia runs regular limited-time events with exclusive rewards — outfits, furniture, emotes, and rare currencies. This guide covers every major event from launch through May 2026, including walkthroughs for the most complex ones.

---

## Winter Frost Season

**Dates**: January 31 – March 13, 2026  
**Requirements**: DG Level 7

The first major seasonal event. Snow covers the entire island, Onsen Mountain transforms into a winter sports hub, and exclusive winter recipes become available.

### Activities

| Activity | Location | Details |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ice Skating | Onsen Mountain (north) | Timed button presses to build Combo Energy for impressive move chains. Two-person skating added March 2026. |
| Snow Slides | Onsen Mountain | Ride snow slides down the mountain |
| Snowman Building | Town areas | Build snowmen for rewards |
| Winter Recipes | Kitchen | Seasonal cooking recipes (Frosted Pancake, Iced Drink) |
| Frostspore Infection Quest | Coastal areas | Canvas coastal areas, get stamps from NPCs and players |

## Snow Concert

**Dates**: During Winter Frost Season  
**Location**: Onsen Mountain summit (near Old Sea) — portal opens to concert venue  
**Host**: Atara

### Schedule

| Period | When |
| --- | --- |
| Opening Week | Daily at 19:00 game time |
| Weekends (from Feb 7) | Saturday & Sunday at 18:10 CT |
| Closing Week | Daily at 19:00 game time |

Runtime: approximately 1 hour 50 minutes, with music and fireworks every 10 minutes.

### Activities at the Concert

- Buy concert merchandise (glow sticks)
- Chat with friends at tables
- Ice skate on the rink
- Collect concert bubbles (sparks, batons, sheet music, gold, Fashionwave Tokens)
- Play your own music during the "free music" segment

### Concert Rewards

Completing personal and team goals rewards **Penguin Gacha** items. The concert also features NPC musicians: **Beau, Muffy, and K.K. Slider**.

## Wonder Egg Hunt (Winter)

**Dates**: During Winter Frost Season  
**Location**: Onsen Mountain

### How It Works

1. Talk to **Eric** to begin Winter Frost quests
2. Speak with **Naughty** on Onsen Mountain to unlock the egg hunt
3. **16 eggs total** — one new egg spawns daily at 6:00 AM
4. Eggs are hidden on fences, statues, inside the igloo, behind stands, on rocks, etc.
5. You **can catch up** on missed eggs from previous days

### Egg Hunt Rewards

Per egg found: **5 Lucky Stars + 300 Fashionwave Tokens** (chance-based payout).

## Fashionwave

**Dates**: Running during Winter Frost and beyond  
**Location**: Onsen Mountain (Azure's shop)

Fashionwave is Heartopia's recurring fashion challenge system:

- Earn **Fashionwave Tokens** via daily logins, weekly challenges, and festival tasks
- Tokens buy exclusive outfits, the **Fashionwave Penguin Set**, vehicles, poses, and furniture
- **Exhibition Passes** unlock premium reward tiers
- Weekly fashion challenges with community voting

### Fashionwave Rewards

- Exclusive winter outfits and accessories
- Fashionwave Penguin Set
- Vehicles and poses
- Building materials
- Steel Tongue Drum instrument skin

## Beaver Build Challenge

**Dates**: April 25 – June 20, 2026  
**Requirements**: DG Level 12, 1,500+ Home Evaluation Score

Team-based building competition:

### How It Works

| Phase | Activity |
| --- | --- |
| **Recruitment** | Form a team of up to 4 players |
| **Creative Period** | Build according to the challenge theme |
| **Exhibition** | Submit your build for community viewing |
| **Selection & Rewards** | Winners chosen, rewards distributed |

### Event Currency

**Fresh Sprouts** — earned during the event, spent on exclusive building materials and decor.

### Tips

- Form a team with players who have complementary building styles
- Use a dedicated blueprint slot for your challenge build
- Coordinate via Discord or in-game chat
- Check the theme requirements carefully before building

## Aurora Banquet Quest

An event quest unlocked during Winter Frost:

1. Talk to the quest giver
2. Craft an **Iced Drink** and **Frosted Pancake** (winter recipes)
3. Serve them at the banquet
4. Rewards include exclusive winter decor

### Frosted Pancake Recipe

Check your recipe book during Winter Frost for the exact ingredients. Winter recipes are only cookable during the season.

## How to Never Miss an Event

1. **Check the Events page daily** — tap the planet icon on your smartwatch
2. **Watch your mailbox** — event announcements arrive as letters
3. **Follow official Heartopia social media** — Twitter/X, Facebook, Discord
4. **Check the weather forecast** — some events tie to specific weather (meteor showers, rainbows)
5. **Keep your DG level current** — most events have DG level requirements
