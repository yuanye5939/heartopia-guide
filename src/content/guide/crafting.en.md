Crafting is unlocked early in Heartopia via **Bob** the handyman. You craft items at a **Workbench**. This guide covers every recipe, required materials, and crafting strategy.

---

## How to Unlock Crafting

1. Meet **Bob** (tutorial, first 30 minutes)
2. He teaches you to craft a **Joinery Single Bed**
3. From then on, use any **Workbench** in town or craft your own

### How to Get a Workbench

You start with access to Bob's workbench. To place your own:

| Item | Recipe | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Workbench | 7 Timber + 2 Quality Timber + 2 Ore | Your own crafting station at home |

## Essential Crafting Recipes

### Repair Kits

| Item | Recipe | Effect | Craft Time |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Repair Kit | 5 Branches | Restores 3% tool durability | 17 sec |
| Crafty Repair Kit | 1 Roaming Oak Timber + 3 Ore | Restores 5% durability + possible temp buff | 20 sec |

> **Important**: Always equip the tool you want to repair **before** using the kit. Using it on the wrong tool wastes the kit.

### How to Use Repair Kits

1. Equip the broken tool in your hand
2. Select Repair Kit from inventory
3. Place it on the ground near your character
4. A sparkle effect appears — tool is restored. Kit is consumed.

## Cooking Stand Equipment

To set up an efficient cooking station at home:

| Item | Source | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Stove | Bob's Furniture Store | ~1,000 Gold each |
| 4-Stove Square Setup | Buy 4 stoves, arrange in square | ~4,000 Gold total |

Standing in the middle of a 4-stove square lets you manage all 4 simultaneously for batch cooking.

## Crafting Strategy

### Early Game
1. **Craft Repair Kits** from Branches — never buy them early when branches are free
2. **Craft Bait** from Apples — speeds up fishing income
3. **Craft Fertilizer** once you have Weeds built up

### Mid Game
1. **Build a Workbench at home** — saves trips to Bob's
2. **Set up a 4-stove kitchen** — batch cooking is your gold engine
3. **Craft windows, doors, and fences** as you unlock blueprints

### Late Game
1. **Crafty Repair Kits** using Roaming Oak Timber — full repair + buff
2. **Premium furniture** for home evaluation score (needed for Beaver Build Challenge)
3. **Complete furniture sets** for bonuses
