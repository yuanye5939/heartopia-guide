Building and decorating your home is one of Heartopia's deepest systems. This guide covers advanced techniques — blueprint loadouts, co-building safely, DIY furniture hacks, and wallpaper tricks you won't find in the tutorial.

---

## Blueprint System (DG Level 8)

Blueprints are essentially **save slots for your house**. You can save multiple versions of your home and switch between them instantly without tearing anything down.

### How Blueprints Work

- Unlocked at **DG Level 8** via the smartwatch app
- Each blueprint slot stores a **complete copy of your house layout**
- Switch between builds freely — nothing is lost
- Blueprint mode gives you **unlimited furniture and decor** to experiment with

### Recommended Blueprint Setup

| Slot | Use |
| --- | --- |
| Slot 1 | Your main home build |
| Slot 2 | Alternative design (e.g., seasonal theme) |
| Slot 3 | **Dedicated testing slot** — experiment freely here |
| Slot 4 | **Co-Build slot** — grant friend permissions only here |

### Blueprint Safety Rule

**Never give a friend build permissions on your main house slot.** Use a dedicated throwaway blueprint slot for co-building so a friend can't accidentally (or deliberately) wreck your real home.

## Co-Building (DG Level 8)

Unlocked alongside Blueprints at DG Level 8. Invite another player to build together in **real-time**.

### How to Co-Build

1. Create a dedicated blueprint slot for co-building
2. Invite a friend from your Friendship Journal
3. Grant build permissions **only on that slot**
4. Both players can place, move, and rotate furniture simultaneously

### Co-Build Safety Checklist

- Use a throwaway blueprint slot — never your main
- Only co-build with trusted friends
- Take a screenshot of your build before starting
- You can revoke permissions at any time

## Custom Furniture Hacks

Why buy expensive furniture when you can build it from basic materials? These DIY tricks save thousands of Gold.

### DIY Shelving

Stack **painted floor panels** parallel against a wall. Use multiple panels to create multi-tier shelving — perfect for displaying collectibles.

### DIY Dining Table

1. Shrink **two columns** to minimum height (table legs)
2. Place them where you want the table
3. Cover with **3 marble or wood floor panels** as the tabletop

### DIY Kitchen Island

Build entirely from **half walls + floor panels**. Much cheaper than buying a kitchen island from Bob's shop, and fully customizable with your own colors and materials.

### DIY Display Pedestals

Place **short columns** indoors to elevate and showcase decorations, trophies, or rare collectibles. Paint them to match your room.

## Wallpaper Tricks

### Mix & Match Wallpaper Patterns

You can combine patterns from different wallpapers on the same wall:

1. Place a **low wall** section
2. Add **two half walls** above it
3. Paint each section with a **different wallpaper**
4. This lets you use the baseboard from one wallpaper and the pattern from another

### Expand Walls Without Repainting

In **overhead mode**, drag an existing wall or floor tile to expand it — the paint and pattern carry over automatically. No need to manually reapply.

## Exterior Materials Indoors

### Built-In Aquarium Display

Use **square wooden windows** to create stunning built-in aquariums:

1. Build a **low-wall platform** where you want the display
2. Place aquariums on top of the platform
3. Enclose with wall panels
4. Frame with **wooden square windows** on both sides
5. The result is a see-through aquarium built into the wall

## Builder Tablet Tips

The Builder Tablet (accessed via your smartwatch) is the precision building tool:

- **Grid view** for pixel-perfect furniture placement
- **Retrieve clipped items** — furniture stuck inside walls that you can't click manually
- **Rotate and move** furniture without picking it up
- **Environment Adjustment Tool** — preview your build in different lighting, weather, and time of day. Windows change color with lighting, so check before finalizing.

- Level up your DG via daily Resident Requests
- Plot expansions are automatic on reaching the required DG level
- Each new plot lets you build additional structures or expand your garden
