Every resource in Heartopia organized by type, with exact spawn locations, daily limits, and best farming routes. Use this guide to find whatever you need for crafting, cooking, or quests.

## Timber & Wood

| Resource | Where to Find | Daily Limit | Used For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Branch** | Harvest from bushes everywhere | Unlimited | Basic crafting, Repair Kits (5 per kit) |
| **Timber** | Chop standard trees (all regions) | Unlimited | Core furniture, building foundations |
| **Quality Timber** | Palm trees on beaches, thick trees in Forest | ~10-15/day | Upgraded furniture, Astralis quest (Bill) |
| **Rare Timber** | 8 Giant Trees around town (2 per corner), giant trees between Plots 4-5 in Suburb | 8/day | Premium furniture, advanced recipes |
| **Roaming Oak Timber** | Oak-Oak NPC (random daily spawn in wild areas: Forest edges, mountains, player gardens) | 3/day | High-end furniture, garden decor, Crafty Repair Kit |
| **Black Walnut** | Black Walnut trees in northeast plains | 3-5/day | Luxury furniture |
| **Bamboo** | Forest regions | ~10/day | Asian-themed furniture, fences |

### Rare Timber Farming Route

1. Check all 8 Giant Tree locations (2 in each corner of town)
2. Search for Oak-Oak in wild areas — use a vehicle for faster scanning
3. Hit palm trees along beaches for Quality Timber

## Ore & Stone

| Resource | Where to Find | Daily Limit | Used For |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Stone** | Mine near home plots, roads, cliffs | Unlimited | Foundations, pathways, basic recipes |
| **Ore** | Mine from pointy rock formations (stone nodes) | ~20/day | Core crafting, upgrades, Astralis quest (Bill) |
| **Coal** | Mine inside caves | ~10/day | Smelting fuel |
| **Flawless Fluorite** | Post-Astralis quest: spawns in players' front gardens (Suburban Ringroad). Glowing blue crystal on tan rocks. | 3/day | Premium lighting, decor, Astralis quest (Eric) |
| **Starfall Shard** | Meteorite ore during Meteor Shower events | Event-limited | Advanced special crafting |

### Flawless Fluorite Daily Farming

After completing the Astralis in Onsen Mountain quest:

1. Fluorite **no longer spawns at Stonehenge**
2. It appears in **other players' front gardens** around the Suburban Ringroad
3. Look for a **glowing, moving blue rock** emerging from a tan rock — it wiggles when you approach
4. Mine with Pickaxe × 3 hits
5. Capped at **3 pieces per day**
6. Check the official Discord's "Daily Fluorite and Roaming Oak" thread for shared coordinates

## Mushrooms

| Mushroom | Region | Spawn Locations |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Button Mushroom** | Flower Field | Next to bushes and fences |
| **Shiitake** | Fishing Village, Flower Field | Under Oak trees, near telephone poles and wooden fences |
| **Penny Bun** | Forest | Base of Pine Trees and fallen logs |
| **Oyster Mushroom** | Onsen Mountain | Foot of rock formations (look for orange rocks) |
| **Black Truffle** | Forest Island | Dirt mounds. 4 spawns, 15-minute respawn. Sells for high price. |
| **Matsutake** | Spirit Oak Pine Forest | Hidden under trees. Rare spawn. |

### Mushroom Cooking Tip

Cook with **4 identical mushrooms** (e.g., 4 Oyster Mushrooms) to unlock secret recipe variants like "Grilled Oysters" instead of generic "Grilled Mushrooms."

## Fish (Key Species)

| Fish | Location | Time | Value |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Crucian Carp** | Spring Lake | All day | ~80g raw / ~200g cooked |
| **Rainbow Fish** | Starlight Sea | Rainbow weather | Very high (premium bait recommended) |
| **Mermaid Fish** | Sea Fishing event | Event only | High value + event currency |

### Fishing Tips

- Use **bait** (3 Apples per craft) for faster catches
- Sea Fishing (DG Level 7, 2,000 Gold) yields the highest-value species
- Match fish to weather and time of day for rare catches
- Always cook fish before selling unless the raw value exceeds the cooked dish price

## Insects (Key Species)

Insects are caught with the Bug Catching hobby (DG Level 5+). Common spawn areas:

| Insect | Region | Time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Butterflies** | Flower Field | Daytime |
| **Beetles** | Forest | Night |
| **Dragonflies** | Near water | Daytime |
| **Fireflies** | Suburban areas | Night |

Insects can be sold to Albert Jr. or used in specific recipes.

## Birds (Key Species)

Birdwatching (DG Level 5+) lets you collect bird cards. Turn in **5 bird cards/day to Bailey J** for repair kits and materials. Bird species vary by region and time of day.
