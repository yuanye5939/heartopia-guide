Heartopia is a free-to-play multiplayer life-simulation game developed by XD Entertainment, released globally on January 17, 2026. Think _Animal Crossing_ meets _Palia_ — you build a cozy life on a charming island with hobbies, home decoration, and multiplayer. This beginner guide covers everything you need to know from Day 1 through endgame unlocks.

## Getting Started

### Character Creation

When you first launch Heartopia, you customize your character's appearance — skin tone, facial features, hairstyle, and outfit. **All of these are purely cosmetic** and can be changed later at Dorothee's Clothing Shop. Nothing you choose here affects gameplay.

### First 30 Minutes

1. **Talk to Annie** at the spawn point — she's the first NPC you meet. She helps you choose a house style and signs you up for the D.G. (Developers Guild).
2. **Meet Bob** the handyman — he unlocks **Crafting** and teaches you to make a Joinery Single Bed.
3. **Craft a Repair Kit** (5 Branches from bushes) — use it to fix Vanya's broken fishing rod.
4. **Catch 3 fish** with Vanya to complete the fishing tutorial.
5. **Visit the Town Centre** — help Dorothee set up the Clothing Store.
6. **Join the D.G. Member's Guild** via Annie — this is your central progression hub.

### Priority #1: Main Story Quests

The main story quests unlock roughly **90% of core features**, including all hobbies, the Builder Tablet, and co-building. They also reward essential currencies (Gold, Contribution Badges). Push the main story before wandering off to explore.

## D.G. (Developers Guild) Level System

The D.G. Member's Guild is Heartopia's **central progression system**. It gates everything — hobbies, home plots, events, crafting recipes, shops, and quality-of-life features.

### How to Level Up

- Earn **Contribution Medals** by completing **Resident Requests** (max 5 per day = 50 medals/day)
- Requests reset at **6:00 AM server time** daily
- Urgent requests (fire icon) pay **50% more** rewards
- **The D.G. system is time-gated** — you cannot grind infinitely. Missing daily requests delays everything by 24 hours.

## Hobbies Unlock Order

Heartopia has **7 hobbies** total. You unlock them with **Hobby Expansion Tickets** earned from DG levels.

| Ticket | DG Level | Hobby | Mentor | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1st | 2 | Fishing | Vanya | Mandatory |
| 2nd | 3 | Gardening | Blanc | Mandatory |
| 3rd | 5 | Cooking | Massimo | **Recommended first** for gold |
| 4th | 8 | Insect Catching | Naniwa | 2nd priority |
| 5th | 11 | Birdwatching | Bailey J | 3rd priority |
| 6th | 12 | Cat Caring | Mrs. Joan | Prerequisite for Dog |
| 7th | 12 | Dog Caring | Mrs. Joan | Requires Cat first |

### Best Hobby Unlock Order

1. **Fishing** → forced at Level 2, strong early income
2. **Gardening** → forced at Level 3, feeds cooking ingredients
3. **Cooking** → pick at Level 5, **best money maker in the game**
4. **Insect Catching** → pick at Level 8
5. **Birdwatching** → pick at Level 11 (least economic value, save for last)
6. **Cat Caring** → Level 12
7. **Dog Caring** → Level 12

### Hobby Upgrade Priority

Save Hobby Upgrade Tickets for the "Big Three" first: **Fishing → Gardening → Cooking**. They drive your entire economy. Bug catching, birdwatching, and pet care can wait.

## How to Earn Gold Fast

### Early-Game Gold Methods

| Method | Profit | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Fruit Jam** | 160-300g each | Forage wild fruits → cook into jam → sell to Albert Jr. |
| **Fried Crucian Carp** | ~200g each | Fish at Spring Lake (all day) → cook → sell |
| **Grilled Mushrooms** | ~150g each | Forage Penny Bun, Shiitake, Button mushrooms → cook |
| **Daily Requests** | 1,000-3,000g | Complete 5 resident requests (urgent = 50% bonus) |

### Mid-Game Gold Methods

| Method | Profit | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Corn Soup** | ~400g each | Plant corn (12h overnight) → cook into soup |
| **Strawberry Jam** | Up to 12,640g at 5-star | Gardening Level 6+, use Fertilizer for quality |
| **Fish & Chips** | ~300g each | Fish + potato → cook. Set up 4 stoves for batch cooking |
| **Repair & Resell** | ~1,400g profit | Buy damaged furniture (~500g) → repair with glue (~100g) → sell (~2,000g) |

### Quick Gold Run (~15 minutes, 8k-12k gold)

1. Plant your most expensive seeds at home
2. Forage all fruit bushes while crafting bait and repair kits
3. Cook all fruit into jam (4 stoves arranged in a square = manage all at once)
4. Fish at a prime spot
5. Sell everything to Albert Jr. in one burst

### Golden Rule

**Never sell raw if cooking doubles the value.** But check your math — a 3-star fish worth 320g shouldn't go into a dish that only sells for 310g.

## Stamina Management

- **Sleep** in your bed for fastest recharge (1 stamina per 5 seconds)
- **Cooked dishes** restore significantly more than raw food
- Energy restoration bubbles exist along mountain paths and event areas
- Plan activities around your stamina pool early on — don't waste it on non-productive actions

## Tool Durability & Repair

Every tool (Watering Can, Pickaxe, Fishing Rod) has a durability bar. When broken, effectiveness drops to near zero.

### Repair Methods

| Method | Cost | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Craft Repair Kit** | 5 Branches (free) | Craft at any Workbench |
| **Buy Repair Kit** | 200 Gold | Ka Ching's General Store |
| **Crafty Repair Kit** | 2 Wishing Stars | Full repair + possible temporary buff |

### How to Use a Repair Kit

**Equip the broken tool in your hand first**, then use the Repair Kit. If you don't have the tool equipped, the kit will be wasted.

## Daily Routine Checklist

Do these every day for maximum progress:

1. **Accept & complete all 5 Resident Requests** (prioritize fire-icon urgent ones)
2. **Check your mailbox** for event rewards and friend gifts
3. **Check the Events page** (planet icon) for sign-in rewards and freebies
4. **Tend your garden** — harvest, replant, water, fertilize immediately after planting
5. **Find Oak-Oak** for 3x Roaming Oak Timber (daily spawn, random wild areas)
6. **Find Flawless Fluorite** for 3x Fluorite (daily spawn, check player gardens)
7. **Mine Rare Timber** from 8 Giant Trees around town (once daily)
8. **Visit shops** — furniture and clothing restock daily
9. **Spend time on hobbies** — cooking is your primary gold engine
10. **Forage and collect** — grab all fruit, branches, mushrooms as you travel

## Multiplayer: How It Works

- Up to **11 other players** visible at any time
- Unlock the **Friendship Journal** at DG Level 4
- Add friends via UID (costs 1 Wishing Star to send a request)
- Visiting a friend's town is **free**
- **Coffee Umbrellas** — chat freely with up to 3 others, play puzzles or fishing mini-games
- **Co-Build** unlocks at DG Level 8 — build together in real-time
- Chat is **only with confirmed friends** — no forced public chat
- **Cross-platform**: mobile and PC players play together (data syncs across devices)

## Common Beginner Mistakes

1. **Skipping daily requests** — you can't catch up; every missed day delays all unlocks by 24 hours
2. **Selling raw instead of cooking** — raw crops and fish sell for pennies; cooked dishes sell for hundreds
3. **Wasting repair kits** — always equip the tool you want to repair before using a kit
4. **Buying expansions at full price** — wait for Ka Ching's 60% discount rotation
5. **Not using a vehicle** — you start with a free scooter; it cuts travel time in half
6. **Ignoring the weather** — meteor showers and rainbows spawn rare resources and unlock Doris
7. **Fishing randomly** — target specific locations, times, and weather for valuable catches
