The **Astralis quest chain** is Heartopia's main storyline. You'll explore four regions, meet key NPCs, and unlock daily rare resource farming. This walkthrough covers every step from triggering the quest to the final reward.

## Prerequisites

Before the Astralis chain begins, you need:

| Requirement | How to reach |
| --- | --- |
| **DG Level 6** | Complete daily Resident Requests for Contribution Medals |
| **A vehicle** | Free scooter from Andrew (test-drive it to unlock the store) |
| **One hobby chosen** | Cooking recommended for energy restoration |

Once at DG Level 6, check your **mailbox** — a mysterious anonymous letter arrives, kicking off the entire Astralis storyline.

## Part 1: Astralis in Forest

**Area**: Northeastern Forest  
**Key NPCs**: Andrew → Patti → Cassie → Oak-Oak  
**Reward**: 3x Roaming Oak Timber + daily Oak-Oak access

### Walkthrough

1. **Meet Andrew** at the quest marker in the northeastern Forest area. He explains Astralis — the mysterious force that brought you to Heartopia.

2. **Find Patti** (forest ranger). She asks you to take a photo of the forest. Climb the observation deck stairs, open your camera, and snap the photo.

3. **Talk to the Bird Lover** — give her some **Timber** (common wood from any tree).

4. **Talk to the Backpacker** — give him **Branches** in exchange for clues about Cassie's location.

5. **Find Cassie** at the **Jump Puzzle area** deep in the Forest. She's on a suspended bridge in the trees.

6. **Cassie's Challenges:**
   - Find hidden "bubbles" (interactive sparkles) around the Forest
   - The second bubble is higher up — **hold the jump button longer** to reach it
   - Complete the jump puzzle for a gift

7. **Cassie's Riddle** to find Oak-Oak:
   > "Cross the lonely bridge, step into the forest's heart. Between two teardrops' gleam, my energetic friend is trapped in hardship."

8. **Solve the riddle**: Cross the same bridge you used to reach Cassie. Look for a clearing between **two glistening ponds**. You'll find **Oak-Oak** — a sentient ancient tree trapped in its own overgrown roots.

9. **Free Oak-Oak**: Equip your **Pickaxe/Axe** and strike the tangled roots until they break. Oak-Oak shakes free and drops your first **3x Roaming Oak Timber**.

10. **Return to Cassie** — quest complete!

### Post-Quest: Daily Oak-Oak Farming

- Oak-Oak spawns **once per day** (resets with server daily reset)
- Yields **3 Roaming Oak Timber** per interaction
- Spawns randomly in **wild areas** (Forest edges, mountains, player house gardens — never in the main city)
- Use your vehicle to do a lap of the map — check residential lots and tree-adjacent areas
- Missed days do NOT roll over

## Part 2: Astralis in Fishing Village

**Area**: Fishing Village  
**Key NPCs**: Martin → Bill  
**Reward**: Quest completion, progress toward Sea Fishing Event

### Walkthrough

1. **Talk to Martin** in the Fishing Village. He directs you to speak with **Bill**.

2. **Bill's Task**: Find two materials:
   - **Quality Timber** — chop trees with your axe. Palm trees on beaches and thick trees in the Forest are best sources.
   - **Ore** — break pointy rock formations using your **Pickaxe**. Look near the Fishing Village area.

3. **Return to Bill** with both materials — quest complete!

> The ore rocks are usually near the Fishing Village, so you won't need to travel far. This is the shortest of the four Astralis quests.

## Part 3: Astralis in Flower Field

**Area**: Flower Fields  
**Key NPCs**: Vernie → Passionate Girl → Teenage Boy  
**Reward**: Quest completion

### Walkthrough

1. **Meet Vernie** in the Flower Fields. She asks you to **find two teenagers**.

2. **Find the Passionate Girl** first:
   - Make sure you have an **instrument** in your backpack
   - Hold the instrument and **play any random tune** for her
   - She'll be satisfied

3. **Find the Teenage Boy**:
   - Same process — equip your instrument and play any tune for him

4. **Return to Vernie** — quest complete!

> You don't need to play a specific tune. Any random performance works for both teenagers.

### Bonus: Flower Field Resources

- **Button Mushrooms** spawn here — look next to bushes and fences
- Great area for foraging common mushrooms to complete Resident Requests

## Part 4: Astralis in Onsen Mountain

**Area**: Adventure Park, Onsen Mountain  
**Key NPC**: Eric  
**Reward**: Recorder + daily Flawless Fluorite access

### Walkthrough

1. **Go to the Adventure Park** on Onsen Mountain and find **Eric** (former theme park manager restoring the mountain).

2. **Eric's Request**: He needs **Flawless Fluorite** — a rare mineral. He hints to **head west up the mountains**.

3. **Follow the mountain path**. Along the way, look for:
   - **Capybara Statues** (landmarks)
   - **Ancient Cave Paintings**
   - **Energy restoration bubbles** — jump to collect these for a stamina boost

4. **Reach the Stonehenge area** — a distinct field with massive stone formations.

5. **Find the Flawless Fluorite**: In the center of Stonehenge, look for a **glowing, moving blue rock** emerging from a tan-colored rock formation. It wiggles as you approach.

6. **Mine it**: Equip your **Pickaxe** and hit it **three times**. After the third strike:
   - The node breaks apart
   - A small mole character with glasses briefly pops out
   - You receive **3x Flawless Fluorite**

7. **Return to Eric** — he rewards you with a **Recorder**.

8. **Return to Andrew** to report everything you've learned — the main quest chain concludes.

### Post-Quest: Daily Flawless Fluorite Farming

- After the quest, Fluorite **no longer spawns at Stonehenge**
- It spawns in **other players' front gardens** in the Suburban Ringroad area
- Look for the same **glowing blue crystal** on tan rocks
- Capped at **3 pieces per day**
- Check the game's **Discord server** for daily shared coordinates ("Daily Fluorite and Roaming Oak" thread)

## After the Main Story

Once all four Astralis quests are complete, prioritize these daily tasks:

1. **Find Oak-Oak** → 3x Roaming Oak Timber (Forest/wild areas)
2. **Find Flawless Fluorite** → 3x Fluorite (player gardens)
3. **Complete 5 Daily Resident Requests** → Contribution Medals
4. **Check Weekly Requests** → additional rewards
5. **Unlock Sea Fishing** at DG Level 7 → talk to Bill at the docks
6. **Keep leveling DG** → Level 12+ unlocks pets, more plots, painting mode, and dreams
