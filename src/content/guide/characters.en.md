Heartopia features **17 NPCs**, each with unique roles — hobby mentors, shopkeepers, quest givers, and event hosts. This guide organizes them by importance tier so you know exactly who to find and when.

---

## S-Tier: Most Essential NPCs

These NPCs are critical to your progression. Visit them daily.

### Vanya — Fishing Mentor

| Detail | Info |
| --- | --- |
| **Location** | Near water bodies around town |
| **Unlock** | DG Level 2 (mandatory) |
| **Role** | Unlocks the **Fishing** hobby, gives you your first fishing rod, teaches bait recipes |
| **Why S-Tier** | Fishing is the strongest early-game income source. Vanya is mandatory — you can't miss him. |

**Services:**
- Gives first Fishing Rod and basic bait recipe
- Teaches fishing mechanics
- Provides apples for bait crafting

---

### Blanc — Gardening Mentor

| Detail | Info |
| --- | --- |
| **Location** | Farm area with planter boxes |
| **Unlock** | DG Level 3 (mandatory) |
| **Role** | Unlocks the **Gardening** hobby, sells seeds and planters |
| **Why S-Tier** | Gardening feeds all cooking recipes. Without Blanc, you have no reliable food source. |

**Shop Inventory:**
- Tomato Seeds, Wheat Seeds, Corn Seeds, Strawberry Seeds
- Planters of various sizes
- Basic Fertilizer

---

### Albert Jr. — Universal Buyer

| Detail | Info |
| --- | --- |
| **Location** | Roams around town — always walking |
| **Role** | Buys everything: fish, crops, bugs, furniture, cooked dishes |
| **Why S-Tier** | He's your primary gold source. Sell everything to Albert Jr. for the best prices. Dreams of building a rocket. |

**What He Buys:**
- All fish (raw or cooked)
- All crops and fruit
- All crafted furniture
- All insect catches
- All cooked dishes

---

### Bob — Furniture & Crafting

| Detail | Info |
| --- | --- |
| **Location** | Town Centre — Furniture Store |
| **Unlock** | Very early (tutorial, before DG) |
| **Role** | Unlocks **Crafting**, sells furniture, blueprints, Wishing Star decor sets |
| **Why S-Tier** | You need Bob for everything related to building and furnishing your home. |

**Shop Inventory:**
- Furniture pieces (rotating daily)
- Blueprint collections (Fences, Windows, Doors, Stairs)
- Wishing Star exclusive furniture sets
- Joinery, Kitchen, Bedroom set pieces

## A-Tier: Very Useful NPCs

Visit these NPCs regularly for specific needs.

### Massimo — Cooking Mentor

| Detail | Info |
| --- | --- |
| **Location** | Town area |
| **Unlock** | DG Level 5, 8, or 11 (choose Cooking with a Hobby Expansion Ticket) |
| **Role** | Unlocks the **Cooking** hobby |

### Ka Ching — General Store

| Detail | Info |
| --- | --- |
| **Location** | Near Suburban Lake |
| **Role** | Sells Repair Kits, Crafty Repair Kits, Writing Desks, Puzzle Sets. **Backpack and Warehouse expansions** (watch for 60% discounts). |
| **Why A-Tier** | The only source of inventory expansions and discount repair kits. Introduces hobbies at tutorial stage. |

**Shop Inventory:**

- Crafty Repair Kit (2 Wishing Stars)

- Warehouse Expansion (25 Wishing Stars — wait for 60% off)
- Writing Desk, Puzzle Sets

## NPC Weather & Time Notes

- **Doris** is the only weather-gated NPC — check your watch's weather forecast weekly
- Most shopkeepers restock **daily at 6:00 AM server time**
- **Albert Jr.** walks circuits around town — if you can't find him, wait at a central intersection
- NPCs do not have closing hours — all are available 24/7 once unlocked
